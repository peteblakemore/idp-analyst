Apply to become an accredited researcher

In order to access legally protected data, you must first become an Accredited Researcher under the Digital Economy Act 2017.

Use this service to:

  • apply to become a full accredited research
  • apply to become a provisional researcher
  • track your application

To get started, you will need to create an account.

Before you start

To be a full accredited researcher you must meet the following criteria:

  • have an undergraduate degree (or higher) including a significant proportion of maths or statistics, or be able to demonstrate at least 3 years quantitative research experience;and
  • have successfully completed a Safe Researcher training course run by ONS, or another equivalent course which has been recognised by ONS such as is run by the UK Data Service, the Administrative Data Research UK partners or HM Revenue and Customs
  • agree to their inclusion in a list of all Accredited Researchers, publishedon the UK Statistics Authority website
  • agree to publish the results of all Accredited Research projects completed as an Accredited Researcher; and
  • sign and adhere to a formal Accredited Researcher Declaration

Researchers who do not meet the first criteria, may apply for Provisional Researcher accreditation.
